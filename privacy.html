Privacy Policy

Welcome to Manalabs8.com (“we,” “us,” or “our”). This policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide: name, email, phone/WhatsApp, company, sector/business context, and message details submitted through forms or direct communication.

Automatically collected information: IP address, browser/device data, pages visited, interaction data, and cookies/related technologies.

2. How We Use Information

  • Respond to inquiries and schedule discovery calls
  • Provide and improve our services
  • Communicate service updates and relevant notices

3. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal information. We may share with trusted service providers (e.g., hosting, email delivery, workflow automation) strictly for service operations, or where legally required.

4. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. No internet transmission or storage method is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

Lead and inquiry data is generally retained for up to 24 months, unless longer retention is required by law or justified by legitimate business need. Data is then deleted or anonymized where practical.

6. Your Rights

Depending on jurisdiction, you may request access, correction, deletion, portability, objection/restriction, or withdrawal of consent. Contact us at privacy@manalabs8.com.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential and basic performance technologies. If we add additional analytics/marketing tools, this policy and notices will be updated.

8. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in jurisdictions where our providers operate. We apply reasonable safeguards consistent with applicable law.

9. Children’s Privacy

Services are not directed to individuals under 18, and we do not knowingly collect children’s personal information.

10. GDPR/CCPA (Where Applicable)

Where GDPR/CCPA applies, you may have additional rights, including complaint rights with relevant authorities. We do not sell personal information.
